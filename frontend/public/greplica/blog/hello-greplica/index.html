Hello Greplica

19 June 2026 · Greplica Team · Company

Welcome to the Greplica blog.

There is no source of truth for all engineering decisions taken in a codebase. Not git, and definitely not your stale Decision Record files.

Thanks to coding agents, it is now possible and we are building it. Greplica.

Despite their ever-growing context windows, coding agents forget. Every session starts from scratch — your trade-offs, your mistakes, your roadmap intentions, all lost unless you manually re-explain them.

Enter Greplica.

Think of it as the advice a principal engineer would give to a junior developer. Or the over-the-shoulder context your colleague gives you when you are reviewing their PR.

It saves the useful context from your coding sessions into a searchable knowledge graph, so your agent starts smarter each time.

What Greplica does

  • Captures decisions, trade-offs, and context from your agent sessions
  • Builds a local knowledge graph of your engineering memory
  • Surfaces relevant context when your agent needs it

No API keys. No cloud. Everything stays on your machine.

Just install Greplica for your repo from here and keep coding. Greplica runs in the background and builds memory as you work.

Questions, bugs, and feedback welcome on or Discord.

Back to work.
